Flora Vista Loyalty Program

At Flora Vista, we appreciate our customers and want to give back for your continued support. Join our loyalty program and earn rewards every time you shop!

How It Works

  • Sign up for free and receive a unique Loyalty ID.
  • For every $1 you spend, you’ll earn 1 point.
  • Once you’ve accumulated 100 points, you’ll receive a $10 discount on your next purchase.
  • Exclusive promotions and offers for loyalty members.

Benefits of Joining

  • Earn Points: Collect points with every purchase.
  • Get Discounts: Use points to receive discounts on future purchases.
  • Exclusive Offers: Receive early access to sales and special offers.
  • Birthday Rewards: Get a special treat on your birthday!

How to Join

Becoming a Flora Vista Loyalty Member is easy! Simply create an account with us, and you’ll automatically be enrolled in our loyalty program. Start earning points with your first purchase.

Check Your Points Balance

Already a member? Log in to your account to check your current points balance and redeem rewards.
